  3. Working at a Bakery

      4. During my high school years, I had the incredible opportunity to work at a local bakery in my hometown, and it was a transformative experience that significantly enriched my baking skills. The bakery became my classroom, and my mentor, a patient and skilled teacher, imparted invaluable knowledge about ingredient ratios, flavor combinations, and the art of precision in pastry-making. From the early morning hours kneading dough to the meticulous process of decorating cakes, every task became a lesson, and every mistake a chance to learn and improve. Through this hands-on experience, I not only honed my technical abilities but also developed a deep appreciation for the dedication and passion required to create delicious baked goods. Working at the bakery not only enhanced my baking skills but also instilled in me a profound love for the art of baking that continues to shape my culinary journey today.
